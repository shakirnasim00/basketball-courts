About

HoopsVictoria focuses on providing effective and useful information on basketball courts in the Greater Victoria Area. We wish to highlight the game of basketball in the capital district, and encourage people to get out and play and engage in the basketball community. We are a non for profit organization, any proceeds made are redistributed into maintaining our site to provide the most accurate and up to date information.
